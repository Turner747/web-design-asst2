A World of Possibilities

Welcome

Gaming has become more and more popular in recent years. Likely due to the rise in 'free-to-play' games such as Fortnite, Apex Legends and Call of Duty Warzone.

While video games first rose to popularity in the 1980s during the arcade era; the invention of the home console and the internet has seen gaming become a sport and a way of life. Gaming tournaments existed during the arcade era, but not on the scale they are today.

Online Gaming

Online gaming really took off during the mid 2000s with the popularity of the Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3 consoles. Both consoles releasing with their own multiplayer networks. This was in the form of Xbox Live and the PlayStation Network, with games such as Call of Duty 4 and Halo 3 giving many players their first taste of online multiplayer gaming.

eSports

eSports have been around since the 90s with strategy games and first-person shooters.

The growth and popularity of eSports has been closely tied with that of online gaming.
