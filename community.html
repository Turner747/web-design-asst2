Get connected

Discord

Discord is an online community platform. It features voice, text and video chat functions; and is commonly used for party chat between players playing together in the same game.

There are thousands of servers dedicated to different games and even hobbies and interest outside of gaming, such as maths and programming.

Get Discord

Twitch

Twitch is an online amateur streaming platform where viewers can watch others play games or stream themselves playing their favourite games.

Twitch is also used to live stream other activities such as woodworking and other hobbies.
