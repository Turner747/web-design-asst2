Games to get lost in

League of Legends

League of Legends is an Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA) game; in which two teams of five champions fight it out for victory on a map of three lanes and a jungle area.

League of Legends has a large community across the world and is one of the most popular games in eSports

Counter-Strike:
Global Offensive

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CSGO) is a first-person shooter in which two teams of six compete in objective based modes on a variety of maps.

The game was released in 2012 but remains one of the most populars games on Steam (videogame marketplace) and in eSports.

DOTA 2

DOTA 2, like League of Legends, is an Multiple Online Battle Arena (MOBA) game; in which two teams of five champions fight it out for victory on a map of three lanes and a jungle area.

DOTA 2 is incredibly popular, often attracting some of the largest prize pool money in eSports.

Rainbow Six: Seige

Rainbow Six: Seige is a recent addition to the eSport scene. It is purpose built eSports game in which two teams of five take each other on in oobjective based modes on a variety of maps; similar to CSGO but with more complex gameplay mechanics.

eSports competions have been held across the globe. The developers continue to support the game with new content periodically.

Fortnite

Fortnite is an incredibly popular 'battle royale' game in which 100 players are dropped onto a map in teams of two, four or as solos, the last player alive wins.

Not only has Fortnite got a huge eSports scene but it has also cemented itself as a pop-culture icon. It features both in many TV shows and movies; and, it features many 'skins' (character models) of popular TV and movie characters.
