PC

The personal computer has been the long standing King of gaming. Due to its long history of performance superiority; it continues to be the platform of choice for power gamers.

Gaming PCs have become their dedicated genre of the PC market. Nearly all PC parts have higher spec gaming counterparts.

Gaming PC are generally on the expensive end of the scale, with high end graphics cards going for $3000+. But entry level gaming PC can be built for between $1200-$1500.

Console

Home gaming consoles first rose to popularity when first released during the 1980s. It wasn't until the release of the Xbox 360 and PS3 in 2007 that online console gaming became widely popular.

The current generation of console, Xbox Series and PlayStation 5, are both currently suffering from supply shortages due to they popularity and COVID supply issues.

Console gaming is much more accessible than PC gaming due the low price point and all inclusive package.
